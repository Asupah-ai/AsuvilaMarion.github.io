Power Bi
Uber Ride Bookings

Created an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze Uber ride bookings (150K bookings, $52M revenue) and track key performance metrics like completion rate (62%), cancellations (25%), and unmet demand (7%).
Cleaned booking data from Kaggle using Power Query to prepare it for analysis.
Built DAX measures to calculate critical KPIs such as revenue, cancellation rates by driver and customer, and average rating.
Designed intuitive visuals showing booking trends, demand fulfillment, and vehicle preferences across rides.
Insights from the dashboard revealed high driver-driven cancellations (18%), unmet demand patterns (7%), and identified the Auto vehicle type as the most booked vehicle (37,419 rides).
Helped demonstrate how data-driven insights can improve service reliability, reduce cancellations, and optimize customer experience in ride-hailing platforms.
